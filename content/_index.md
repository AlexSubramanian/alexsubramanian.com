I'm a senior software engineer at Salesforce, where I design and ship LLM-powered agentic features for Tableau Next — including natural language metric summaries backed by evaluation pipelines and statistical testing frameworks. Before that, I built the core insights microservice for Tableau Pulse, generating trend analysis, forecasting, anomaly detection, and more across enterprise metrics.

I started my career at Boeing — first as an electrophysics engineer on classified programs, then building cockpit displays and embedded software for the F/A-18 and E/A-18G. I moved to Sierra Nevada Corporation where I led a software team on a $24M classified training system for aerospace and defense. From there I joined Narrative Science to work on an AI-driven analytics platform, which was acquired by Salesforce.

I hold an MS in Computer Science with a focus in AI/ML from Arizona State University, and a BS in Electrical Engineering from the University of Missouri.

Outside of work, I run a [self-hosted homelab](/projects/homelab/) and enjoy building infrastructure from scratch — including [this site](/docs/hosting-this-site/).
