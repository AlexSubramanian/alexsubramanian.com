I'm a software engineer at Salesforce building Tableau Next. I started my career at Boeing as an electrophysics engineer on classified programs, then transitioned into software — building cockpit displays and embedded systems for the F/A-18 and E/A-18G. I moved to Sierra Nevada Corporation, where I led a team building mission systems for aerospace and defense. From there I joined Narrative Science to work on an AI-driven analytics platform, which was ultimately acquired by Salesforce.

I hold an MS in Computer Science with an emphasis in Machine Learning from Arizona State University, and a BS in Electrical Engineering from the University of Missouri.

Outside of work, I run a [self-hosted homelab](/projects/homelab/) and enjoy building infrastructure from scratch — including [this site](/docs/hosting-this-site/).
